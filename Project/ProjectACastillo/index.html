Custom health interventions for patients

Naima Health develops health tools using behavioral decision science and machine learning to engage patients in their clinical care



Our Methods


Expert Research

We draw on scientific literature, elicit expert judgments, and employ machine learning to create predictive models.

Participatory Design

We work alongside our target audience to ensure that their preferences and sociocultural contexts are honored.

Prototype + Test

We design and prototype interventions and tools that support patients. We perform rigorous clinical studies.

Roll-Out

We work with our partners to implement the newly-designed interventions, tools and systems.
